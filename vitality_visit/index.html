OUR TEAM

Meredith Grey

Dr. Meredith Grey is the head of General Surgery. She joined us at Vitality Visit as an intern right out of medical school and continues to work. Dr. Grey works with patients of all ages that may need a splenectomy, exploratory gastrointestinal surgery, or etc.

Derek Shepherd 

Dr. Derek Shepherd serves as the Head of Neurosurgery. He has been working at Vitality Visit for 23 years. On top of serving as the Head of Neurosurgery, Dr. Shepherd also serves as a valued member on our board. Shepherd is experienced in brain stimulation surgery, repairs, and is even nationally recognized.

Mark Sloan

Dr. Mark Sloan is the head of Plastic Surgery. He is board certified in Plastics and ENT (Otolaryngology). He has been with us for 17 years and serves on the hospital's board. He works with emergency patients and cosmetic patients. Experienced with many types of reconstructions and repairs.

Amelia Shepherd

Dr. Amelia Shepherd works in our Neurosurgery department as a co-head with Dr. Derek Shepherd. She has been practicing with us for 8 years and has conducted numerous extractions and repairs. She also does her own neurological research on the side.

Cristina Yang

Dr. Cristina Yang is the head of Cardiothoracic surgery. She has been working for 11 years and has conducted research on 3D printed conduit hearts, winning her the Harper Avery Award. She received her Ph.D at UC Berkeley and her MD at Stanford.     

Addison Montgomery

Dr. Addison Montgomery is is a double board-certified OB/GYN, subspecializing in maternal-fetal medicine/fetal surgery, a neonatal surgeon, as well as practicing as a medical geneticist. Additionally, she is our department head for OB/GYN and neonatal.

General Surgery

General surgery is a surgical specialty that focuses on abdominal contents including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, liver, pancreas, gallbladder, appendix and bile ducts, and often the thyroid gland.

Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery is concerned with the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of disorders which affect any portion of the nervous system including the brain, spinal cord, central and peripheral nervous system, and cerebrovascular system

Plastic Surgery

Plastic surgery is a surgical specialty involving the restoration, reconstruction, or alteration of the human body. It can be divided into two main categories: reconstructive surgery and cosmetic surgery.

Cardiothoracic Medicine

Cardiothoracic surgery is the field of medicine involved in the treatment of organs inside the thoracic cavity, lungs (lung disease), and other pleural or mediastinal structures.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics is the branch of medicine that involves the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians provide preventive care and also diagnose and treat infections, injuries and diseases.
